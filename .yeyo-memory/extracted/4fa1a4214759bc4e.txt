1. General 
 
1.1 This specification sets forth acceptable standards of materials for removable and reusable 
blanket, pad and jacket type insulation. This type of insulation shall be used for covering such 
items as vessel and duct manway/access way covers, duct expansion joints and duct blank-offs, 
gates, valves, sight glasses, pumps, etc. 
 
1.2 This specification is an integral part of specifica tion Insulation Subcontract 4521-ESP-ANC-00-62-
0002. 
 
1.3 Material selection, thickness, and design shall be based on the information supplied in 
specification 4521-ESP-ANC-00-60-G115 (later), Paint and Insulation Schedule. 
 
2. Installation 
 
 
See Figure 1 for a typical insulating jacketing installed over manway/access way nozzle. 
 
3. Materials 
 
The insulation characteristics shall meet the minimum requirements of the system being insulated.
