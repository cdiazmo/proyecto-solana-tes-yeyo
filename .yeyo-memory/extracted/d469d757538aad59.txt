6. MECHANICAL & ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT TEMPERATURE RATINGS 
 
6.1 Outdoor Mechanical & Electrical Equipment 
Outdoor mechanical & electrical equipment shall be designed for design dry bulb temperature 
plus 1008W/m2 DNI (Daily Normal Irradiance), unless otherwise stated in mechanical or electrical 
equipment specifications. 
 
6.2 Mechanical & Electrical Equipment in Indoor Non-cooled Buildings (i.e. ventilation fans only 
buildings) 
Building manufacturer shall design ventilation system in order to account for ambient design dry 
bulb temperature plus direct sunlight plus code ca lculated heat gains. Ventilation shall be sized 
to not allow inside building temperature to rise more than ten degrees above ambient design dry 
bulb temperature. Mechanical & electrical equi pment inside these indoor non-cooled buildings 
shall be designed with temperature ratings not less than 149 degrees F.
