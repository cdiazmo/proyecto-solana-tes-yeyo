Notes:
1. FC85 is to be used only in the boiler feedwater line at the inlet to the waste heat boiler as specified on the flow diagrams and at no other location.
2. When flanged gate valves are required for 2” and under use FA84.
3. All piping shall comply with ANSI B31.3 Basic Code (Fabrication & Erection, Section 327; Inspection & Examination, Section 340 & 341) unless otherwise 
specified in the shop fabrication specification.
4. Heat treatment of 10” thru 24” piping required per ANSI/ASME B31.3, Section 331.1.
5. Upon prolonged exposure to temperature above 775 F., the carbide phase of carbon steel may be converted to graphite (ASME B31.1, 1989, Table A-1, Note 
2). Values indicated in pressure/temperature chart (Page 2 of 2) at 800 f. Are per ASME B31.1, 1989, Table A-1 and may be used with extreme caution on a 
case by case basis, but in no case exceed the 800  F. limit.
6. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie-in locations regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting.
7. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per spec 4521-ESP-ANC-00-61-PS010.
8. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum standard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, construction d ebris and foreign objects.
9. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas ( “TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process.
