1. Buttweld piping system intended to be used for connecting to equipment. 
2. Minimum bend radius for tubing in this specification is 2x outside tube diameter. Neither major nor minor diameter of tube should deviate 
from normal by more than 10% per ASTM A536. Recommended minimum bend radius is 3x outside tube diameter. 
3. Pressure / temperature allowed in this spec. are limited by F316 flange material. 
4. All material in this spec is intended to be designed, fabricated and purchased in the field.
