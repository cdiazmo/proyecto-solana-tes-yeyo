Objective
The purpose of this document is to validate the piping layout and define the pipe supports for the lines included in the associated stress calculation models of molten salts and HTF system as a result of the heat exchangers modification. Design validation is obtained by modelling the effect of the external loads applied on the pipeline. The analysis of model shows the potential behaviour of pipe system under application of all external loads. This analysis will provide the following information:
Pipe stress verification for application code.
Calculation of loads and displacements applied on pipe supports for their correct sizing.
This piping dossier for the molten salts and HTF Power block system is composed of these documents and the associated annexes specified in paragraph 8. Mainly these annexes are related to the input echo, the stress report and stress isometrics from Caesar II 2016.
