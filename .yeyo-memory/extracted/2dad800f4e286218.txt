1. VENDOR DATA SUBMITTAL:
 1.1. The following instructions for document transmittal to Abener Teyma shall be 
 observed by all vendors. The required method of sending documents is 
 electronically. Electronic documents must be submitted using the Prolog 
 Website. Vendor should inquire through the Abener Teyma purchasing agent for
 instructions on using the Prolog Website.
 1.2. Vendor is required to comply with these guidelines. Failure to provide 
 documentation or written notification of change request will result in payment 
 delays. Written notification does not alleviate Vendor from compliance with 
 these documents unless approved by Abener Teyma Lead Engineer in writing.
 1.3. Document Requirements
 1.3.1. An electronic transmittal form shall accompany each submission. 
 This transmittal will list each document being submitted.
 1.3.2. Vendor specific information shall be present on transmittal (cover) 
 letters, title pages, and in title blocks or other prominent position on 
 drawings, and shall include the following information:
 a. Purchaser's Corporate Name;
 b. Purchaser's project number;
 c. Equipment item number and service name;
 d. Purchase order number;
 e. Any other identification specified in the contract 
 d ocuments;
 f. Vendor's identifying proposal number, shop order 
 number, serial number, or other reference required to 
 completely identify return correspondence.
 1.3.3. All documents and drawings shall be for the specific equipment or 
 design being purchased from vendor. "Typical" drawings are not 
 preferred. If "typical" drawings are used, they shall be marked up or
 otherwise modified with the specific equipment data.
 1.3.4. All document and drawing files shall have a unique document 
 number and the electronic file (e-file) name shall be ONLY that 
 number followed by the ".pdf" or ".dwg" extension regardless of 
 revision. (i.e. xxxx.pdf)
 1.3.5. All documents created electronically shall be submitted in black and
 white ".pdf" format. Native files converted to ".pdf" shall be forced

 1.4. Drawing Requirements
 1.4.1. Vendor shall certify each drawing as correct, positively identify it as 
 to the equipment to which it applies, and list all changes on each 
 drawing maintaining a date and description of each revision.
 1.4.2. All final drawings shall be sent in ".pdf" and ".dwg" formats for the 
 project. Final drawings not sent in CAD format will result in 
 payment delays.
 1.4.3. Each drawing shall have the correct CAD "paper size" noted on the 
 document. (i.e. 11x17, 8 1/2x11, etc.)
 1.4.4. Vendor shall notify Abener Teyma if any changes are made to the 
 final drawings in writing no more than 5 days after any design 
 change. This letter should explain the change with pictures & 
 drawings and explain why it was necessary. Abener Teyma 
 reserves the right to accept or make modification/corrections to this
 change. Vendor shall be responsible for updating all final 
 manufacturing drawings and incorporating these changes into the 
 as-built drawings.
 1.4.5. Bill of material shall show all OEM names, equipment part 
 numbers, and equipment ratings.

 Documents Requirements.
