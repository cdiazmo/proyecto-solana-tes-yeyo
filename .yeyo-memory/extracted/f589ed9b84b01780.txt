Overview
In the present document, the Drain Back tank pit of Solana Generating Station is designed.
Description
The pit’s shape and the different equipment, pipeline and valves that affect to this one are shown in the next image: 
The Drain back tank pit is composed by walls and slab of foundation, made reinforced concrete, with dimensions: area 24’11”x 15’9”, of inner height of 10’6” and 3’0” of thickness. This pit is projected to support the Drain back tank, pipeline, valves and the integrated pump.
This pit is projected buried and supported over subbase by filling with suitable material, type A1 or A3 compacted 95% in according to AASTHO T1-180. In this sense, it has account the lateral ground load on the structural calculation of pit.
Also it is projected a steel structure to support the drain tank equipment, on the slab and anchoraged by several post-installed bolt to this one. In the document 4521-CAL-AEE-75-0003-GAR it shows the check report.
