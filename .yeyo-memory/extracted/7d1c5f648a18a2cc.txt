(6) According to buyer's General Piping Technical Specification

(8) For a more detailed drawing of this tank see Page 4.
(9) Supplier will include the design and supply of anchor bolts, anchor plate and template for the bases (fix and sliding supports) and Cogetherm insulation.
(10) Supplier will include the design and supply for insulation clips.
(11) The tank will be N2 blanketed during stand-by periods.Also pressurized N2 will be used to empty the tank during drainage operation.
(12) Manhole to be located on top. If there is not enough space, manhole will not be installed and access to the tank will be through the pump hole
(13) To be confirmed by pump supplier
(14) The weld for transportation caps has to be lower that the use for the hydraulic test and has to protect the nozzle mechanized according ASME 16/25
(15) The tank can suffer temperature step changes close to 212ºF up to a maximum of 718ºF.
(17) Tank will include a pit to give submergence to the pump
(16) Tank will suffer 12 thermal cycles per year, from 29ºF to 536ºF and from 536ºF to 750ºF
(17) Refer to the specification 4521-SPE-SGT-000-223-0006 Maximum allowed loads in nozzles.
