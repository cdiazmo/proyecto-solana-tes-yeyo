This specification describes the scope of work for supply of all instruments and 
valves as listed at the attached datasheet for quote needed for the project. 
The attachments identified in this document are con sidered part of this 
specification and Supplier shall be bound to adhere to these specifications. Any

documentation submitted by Supplier.
