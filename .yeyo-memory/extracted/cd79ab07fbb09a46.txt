1. General : 
 
This specification sets forth acceptable standards of materials for mineral fiber insulation. 
 
2. Temperature Range : 
 
This specified insulation, all forms, can be used o n surfaces having operating temperatures up to a 
maximum of 1200 °F (649 °C). 
 
3. Materials 
: 
 
3.1 Mineral Fiber Blanket (Flexible): 
 
 
3.1.1 Insulation shall conform to ASTM Specification C 55 3, Type VII with the following 
additional requirements: 
 
Nominal density: (Verify with Mfr.) 8 lbs/ft 
3 (128 kg/m 3) 
Facing: None 
Cumulative shot content per ASTM C 612: <30% 
Meet requirements of ASTM specification: C 795 (“Th ermal Insulation For 
Use In Contact With Austenitic 
Stainless Steel”) 
 
Insulation shall meet all above requirements for all applications. 
 
3.1.2 Available Sizes : 
 
Length: 24”, 36”, 48”, 96” ( 600 mm, 900 mm, 1200 mm, 2400 mm) 
Width: 12”, 24”, 36”, 48” ( 300 mm, 600 mm, 900 mm, 1200 mm) 
Thickness: 1”- 6” in ½” increments ( 25 mm - 150 mm in 12 mm increments)

3.2 Mineral Fiber Board and Block : 
 
3.2.1 Insulation shall conform to ASTM Specification C 61 2, Type IV with the following 
additional requirements: 
 
Nominal density (Verify with Mfr.) Board: 8 lbs/ft 
3 (128 kg/m 3) 
 Block: 12 lbs/ft 3 (192 kg/m 3) 
Facing: None 
Cumulative shot content per ASTM C 612: <30% 
Compressive Strength-Block @ 10% Deform: 120 lb/ft 2 (0.06 kg/cm 2) 
Meet requirements of ASTM specification: C 795 (“Th ermal Insulation For 
Use In Contact With Austenitic 
Stainless Steel”) 
 
Insulation shall meet all above requirements for all applications. 
 
3.2.2 Available Sizes : 
 
Length: 36”, 48” ( 900 mm, 1200 mm) 
Width: 24”, 36”, 48” (600 mm, 900 mm, 1200 m m) 
Thickness: 1” - 5” in ½” increments (25 mm - 125 mm in 12 mm increments)

3.3 Loose Fill Mineral Fiber : 
 
Insulation shall conform to ASTM Specification C 76 4, Type II (poured application) with the 
following additional requirements: 
 
Nominal density (Verify with Mfr.): 8 - 16 lbs/ft 
3 (128 - 256 kg/m 3) 
Meet requirements of ASTM specification: C 795 (“Th ermal Insulation For Use In 
Contact With Austenitic Stainless Steel”) 
Packaging: Packaged in manufacturer’s standard 
containers 
 
Insulation shall meet all above requirements for all applications.
