1. General 
1.1 This specification describes the scope of work for supply of all instruments as listed in this request for quote needed for the project. 
1.2 Definitions 
1.2.1 "Supplier" shall mean the instrument supply company chosen for the project. 
1.2.2 "Customer" shall mean Abener Teyma. 
1.2.3 "Fabricator" or "fabricators" shall mean any instrument assembly shop subcontracted by Abener Teyma (if applicable). 
1.2.4 "Owner" shall mean Abengoa Solar Inc. 
1.3 The attachments identified in this document are considered part of this specification and Supplier shall be bound to adhere to these 
specifications. Any specifications attached shall be met and followed by Supplier in full. All drawings and documents included in this package are 
considered "Contract Documents". 
1.4 The imperative language of this specification is directed to the Supplier, unless specifically noted otherwise. Supplier shall perform all work 
described unless other parties are specifically identified as performing the work. All directives such as "provide", "install", "perform", "test", etc., 
are addressed solely to the Supplier, whether or not the word "Supplier" is specifically mentioned. Work included for others will be specifically 
identified. 
1.5 English/ U.S. units shall be used on all drawings, calculations and any other documentation submitted by Supplier. 
2. Scope 
2.1 Scope of this contract is supply of instruments as outlined in the following engineering specifications required for the project including all 
cables, gasket, manifolds … etc as identified in the main specification as options or accessories. Customer plans to establish unit pricing for 
material to be used throughout the duration of the project. Quantity of instruments needed for the contract should be identified on the main lead 
engineering specification. Shipping schedule to be confirm with purchasing during the final commercial agreement 
2.2 Packaging - To be defined later. 
2.3 Labeling, tagging: Specific labeling /tagging requirements to be defined later. 
2.4 Supplier shall be responsible for delivery of all material to Abener Teyma selected Fabricator(s) and/or project jobsite. Fabricator(s) will notify 
Supplier of material required on an as needed basis. Shipments to Fabricator or jobsite should be scheduled to ensure a full load will be delivered 
in order to reduce transportation costs.

2.6 Supplier shall provide a single contact person to monitor and track all material purchased under this contract. This person should be available 
for update meetings in Abener Teyma St. Louis office (if necessary) and/or via teleconference. It is the Supplier's sole responsibility to track and 
document all material within the scope of this contract.

Specific Instructions to Vendors 
4.1. To furnish a quote for a design, manufacture, assemble, test and deliver to destination the equipment and materials called out for in accordance 
with the following. 
4.2. Note to Vendor: Quotes are due by noon CST on the inquiry due date. 
4.3. Prices quoted on this proposal shall be firm and not subject to escalation on either labor or material. Bidders are to provide best price and 
delivery in the quote submitted by their company against this inquiry. Based on the commercial and technical information received during the 
competitive bid process a final vendor/contractor selection shall be made. All pricing shall be considered final. 
4.4. The due date shown on this request for quotation is important. Your failure to respond in time could result in your being eliminated from 
consideration. If you are unable to meet the due date requirement or if you are unable to submit a quotation, notify the buyer. 
4.5. Your quotation may be used to purchase on an " Any or All" basis. If pricing is different for either situation, provide pricing on both. 
4.6. Technical/commercial qualifications must be clearly stated. Bidders are encouraged to propose alternate offerings which clearly demonstrate a 
cost or performance advantage. Sales and Use tax to be shown separately. 
4.7. Vendor shall complete and return the vendor proposal price data sheet and the vendor data requirements specification with their proposal. 
Failure to return these completed documents may have an impact on proposal consideration. 
4.8. Furnish electronic quotation. Any exceptions to the specifications must be clearly indicated in your quotation. Quoted price must be firm 
through the quoted delivery. All price quotations are required to be valid for acceptance for 90 days after RFQ date.

exceptions to the inquiry documents must be clearly stated in seller's quotation. Absence of such specific and individual objections and/or exceptions 
shall constitute acceptance of, and intention of compliance with, all inquiry documents. 
4.10. Technical questions regarding this request for quotation should be directed to Tim Tran at 314-275-1151 - cell 314-277-5435 or e-mail 
timothy.tran@abener.abengoa.com. NOTE - The buyer should be included in all correspondence between the vendor and the engineer. 
4.11. Successful bidder will be required to comply with attached vendor data distribution and vendor drawing title block information. 
4.12. Successful vendor will be required to furnish drawings, instruction manuals, and other information as indicated on the attached "Vendor Data 
Requirements" form.
