1. General
1.1 This specification describes the scope of work for supply of all instruments as listed in this request for quote needed for the project.
1.2 Definitions
1.2.1 "Supplier" shall mean the instrument supply company chosen for the project.
1.2.2 "Customer" shall mean Abener Teyma.
1.2.3 "Fabricator" or "fabricators" shall mean any instrument assembly shop subcontracted by Abener Teyma (if applicable).
1.2.4 "Owner" shall mean Abengoa Solar Inc.
1.3 The attachments identified in this document are considered part of this specification and Supplier shall be bound to adhere to these specifications.
Any specifications attached shall be met and followed by Supplier in full. All drawings and documents included in this package are considered
Contract Documents.
1.4 The imperative language of this specification is directed to the Supplier, unless specifically noted otherwise. Supplier shall perform all work
described unless other parties are specifically identified as performing the work. All directives such as "provide", "install", "perform", "test", etc.,
are addressed solely to the Supplier, whether or not the word "Supplier" is specifically mentioned. Work included for others will be specifically
identified.
1.5 English/ U.S. units shall be used on all drawings, calculations and any other documentation submitted by Supplier.
2. Scope
2.1 Scope of this contract is supply of instruments as outlined in the following engineering specifications required for the project including all cables,
gasket, manifolds … etc as identified in the main specification as options or accessories. Shipping schedule to be confirm with purchasing during the
final commercial agreement
2.2 Packaging - To be defined later.
2.3 Labeling, tagging: Specific labeling /tagging requirements to be defined later.
2.4 Supplier shall be responsible for delivery of all material to Abener Teyma selected Fabricator(s) and/or project jobsite. Fabricator(s) will notify
Supplier of material required on an as needed basis. Shipments to Fabricator or jobsite should be scheduled to ensure a full load will be delivered in
order to reduce transportation costs.
2.5 The following items are excluded from this subcontract:
"
"
2.6 Supplier shall provide a single contact person to monitor and track all material purchased under this contract. This person should be available for
update meetings in Abener Teyma St. Louis office (if necessary) and/or via teleconference. It is the Supplier's sole responsibility to track and
document all material within the scope of this contract.

3. Quality
3.1 All material shall be supplied to meet all dimensional, quality, marking and stamping, and all other requirements for all NEC, IEEE, UL …. etc
standards listed in instrument specifications. Any material not meeting these standards will be returned and replaced at Supplier's expense.
Notice:
o Your commitment to supply all the required vendor data as promised is crucial to the progress of this project. The attached vendor data requirements
reflects when we need our documentation. Please complete the second column of this form entitled "Vendor Commitment" showing number of weeks
after order for date of submittal, and include with your proposal. Failure to comply may result in rejection of your bid.
o Your quotation must state time required to submit approval drawings and the time required to fabricate after drawing approval is received.
o Equipment is to be delivered to purchaser's manufacturing site with specific locations to be released later.
o Shipment is required by a strict delivery schedule to be finalized at the time the final contract is materialized, the best estimated shipping date 
July 2012 
o For the equipment on this quote, a list of recommended spare parts for one year's normal maintenance with price and delivery for each item is
required. Also indicate any savings or discounts which may be offered if the spares are purchased for delivery with the equipment order. We need this
information with the quotation, in order for our client to have spares available during start-up and to take advantage of any economies available.
o The equipment and components covered by this quotation, including any field erection that may be involved, will be subject to inspection by Abener
Teyma personnel prior to shipment to assure compliance with specifications.
o Abener Teyma reserves the right to perform inspection activities before and during fabrication, and prior to shipment, if desired. 
Contact Rick Carlton, e-mail rick.carlton@abener.abengoa.com at (314) 275-1323 for details.
o We solicit your recommendations that may improve the price, delivery or performance. The vendor is invited to quote as an alternate, changes in
physical dimensions, modifications to the design, prefabrication, stock or manufacturer's standard equipment that would reduce the initial or
maintenance cost and/or improve overall quality of the premise. Alternate quotations shall be listed as such in the proposal specifying the alternate and
price and delivery for that alternate. The purchase will, in general, be made on a basis of adherence to the original design premise, economic value, 
and best delivery.
o The attached specifications were prepared using accepted engineering practices, approved technical standards (ASME, ASTM, AWS, etc.) as well as
meeting EPA Environmental and OSHA safety regulations. It is expected that if, during your review of these specifications, you find needed corrections,
changes, or alterations that you bring these to our attention. Further, if you do not clearly understand the specifications, you must request clarification.
Give priority to pricing standard equipment and components.
o Unless specified to the contrary in the technical specifications, the following preparations for shipment apply:
o After inspection and testing, equipment shall be completely free of water, dried and prepared for shipment. Adequate protection shall be provided
against mechanical damage and atmospheric corrosion in transit, and for at least six (6) months outdoor storage.
o All openings shall be covered, securely fastened and suitable for prolonged exposure prior to final installation.
