Notes:
1. Backfill is to be in full contact with the pipe and compacted to a general density of over 85% of Standard Proctor (AASHTO T-99) 
density. Installation to be in accordance with ASTM D-2321, ASTM D-2774 and “Plastic Pipe Institute Report TR-31/ 9-79.”
2. All underground flanged connections to be tar coated to protect both bolts and back -up flanges from corrosion.
3. All underground valves to be installed with a valve box.
4. All piping to be marked with BLUE stripe for water service, GREEN for wastewater, drain or sewer service .
