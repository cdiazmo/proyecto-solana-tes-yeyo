1. General : 
1.1 This specification sets forth acceptable standards of materials for aluminum jacketing, for use on 
thermal insulation, to provide outdoor weatherproofing for hot surfaces. 
2. Materials : 
2.1 Aluminum Jacketing : 
Jacketing shall conform to ASTM Specification B 209 and be Aluminum alloy Type 3003, 3004, 
3105 or 5005 with H-14 Temper Range, or equivalent, with Stucco embossed surface. All 
jacketing, with the exception of corrugated jacketi ng, shall have a protective backing facing the 
insulation. This acid resistant backing is made of kraft paper with 1 mil polyethylene film. Jacket 
thickness shall be as specified below. 
2.1.1 Vertical Cylindrical Vessels 
: 
(1) Vessel Side - Corrugated sheets of 0.024" minimum thickness. C orrugations 
shall be ¼” deep and 2 ½” (64 mm) across except for small vessels, blow dow n 
tanks for example, which shall have 1 ¼” (32 mm) corrugations. 
(2) Vessel Top - Dished/Conical - Flat sheets of 0.032" (0.8 mm) minimum 
thickness. 
(3) Vessel Top - Flat/Sloped - Flat sheets of 0.032” (0.8 mm) minimum thickness 
2.1.2 Horizontal Cylindrical Vessels : 
(1) Vessel Side - Flat sheets of 0.024” (0.6 mm) minimum thickness . 
(2) Vessel Ends - Flat sheets of 0.032” (0.8 mm) minimum thickness. 
2.1.3 Economizers, Superheaters, and Heat Exchangers : 
Corrugated sheets of 0.024” (0.6 mm) minimum thickn ess. Corrugations shall be 1 ¼” 
(32 mm) across and ¼” (6 mm) deep.

2.1.4 Ducting : 
Flat sheets of 0.024” (0.5 mm) minimum thickness. 
2.1.5 Piping : 
Factory fabricated smooth lengths with longitudinal lock joints and end butt straps, or 
strapping bands as follows: 
(1) Up to 24” (600 mm) diameter - 0.016” (0.4 mm) jacket thickness. 
 
(2) 24” (600 mm) diameter and over - 0.020” (0.5 mm) jacket thickness. 
 
Preformed covers, in thickness as noted above, for fittings shall be utilized wherever 
possible. 
2.1.6 Manhole Covers 
: 
Flat sheets of 0.032” (0.8 mm) minimum thickness. 
2.1.7 Available Sizes : 
(1) Corrugated: 
Length: - standard up to 12 feet (3.6 m) 
- longer sheets with special order. 
Width: - 33”, 44” (825 mm, 1100 mm) 
(2) Flat 
: 
Length: - 100 ft. (30.5 m) rolls 
Width: - 36”, 48” (900 mm, 1200 mm)
