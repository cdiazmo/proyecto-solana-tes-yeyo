Notes: Notes: Notes: Notes: 
1. Lap joint flanges and stub ends are to be used only as required to assure proper bolt-hole alignment a t odd-angle piping configurations. 
2. When flanged gate valves 2” and smaller are require d use FA54. 
3. FC55 is to be used only in the boiler feedwater lin e at the inlet to the waste heat boiler as specifie d on the flow diagrams and at no other location. 
4. All piping shall comply with ANSI B31.3 basic code (fabrication and erection, Section 327; inspection and examination, Section 340 and 341) unless otherw ise 
specified in the shop fabrication specification. 
5. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie -in locations regardless of size change due to diff iculty of realignment of piping after cutting. 
6. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per sp ec 4521-ESP-ANC-00-61-PS010 . 
7. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds sh all be done using Tungsten Inert Gas (“TIG” – “Heli arc”) welding process. 
8. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum s tandard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, co nstruction debris and foreign objects.
