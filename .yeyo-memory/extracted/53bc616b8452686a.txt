Purpose and scope
This document is intended to evaluate the implications of replacing the existing TES System plate heat exchangers, for a new set of heat exchangers of the tube and shell based on Lointek’s .
The new proposed heat exchangers will have different pressure drops and will be located at a different location and at heights of the existing ones. To evaluate the consequences of installing this equipment on the Molten Salts circuits, a set of hydraulic calculations were simulated using Pipeflo ®.The considered cases were the following:
Summer Charging
Summer Discharging
Winter Discharging
Max Charging
25% Charging
Molten Salts Circuit
System description
The plant includes 6 identical and independent molten salts circuits. For simulation purposes the following equipment where considered:
One (1) hot salts tank (1/../6 -MT-252)
One (2) cold salts tank (1/../6 -MT-251)
One (1) HTF-to-Salt Heat Exchanger (1/../6 -MX-251)
One (1) hot salts pump (1/../6 -MP-252)
One (1) cold salts pump (1/../6 -MP-251)
Because there are no differences in terms of geometry and equipment performance, the molten salts circuit was simulated for a single set of tanks for the different operating modes.
