1. VENDOR DATA SUBMITTAL: 
1.1. The following instructions for document transm ittal to Abener Teyma shall be observed by all 
vendors. The required method of sending documents is electronically. Electronic documents must be 
submitted using the Prolog Website. Vendor should inquire through the Abener Teyma purchasing agent for 
instructions on using the Prolog Website. 
1.2. Vendor is required to comply with these guidel ines. Failure to provide documentation or written 
notification of change request will result in payment delays. Written notification does not alleviate Vendor 
from compliance with these documents unless approved by Abener Teyma Lead Engineer in writing. 
1.3. Document Requirements 
1.3.1. An electronic transmittal form shall accompa ny each submission. This transmittal will list each 
document being submitted. 
1.3.2. Vendor specific information shall be present on transmittal (cover) letters, title pages, and in title 
blocks or other prominent position on drawings, and shall include the following information: 
 a. Purchaser's Corporate Name; 
 b. Purchaser's project number; 
 c. Equipment item number and service name; 
 d. Purchase order number; 
 e. Any other identification specified in the contr act documents; 
f. Vendor's identifying proposal number, shop order number, serial number, or other reference 
required to completely identify return correspondence. 
1.3.3. All documents and drawings shall be for the specific equipment or design being purchased from 
 vendor. "Typical" drawings are not preferred. If " typical" drawings are used, they shall be 
marked up or otherwise modified with the specific equipment data. 
1.3.4. All document and drawing files shall have a unique document number and the electronic file 
(e-file) name shall be ONLY that number followed by the ".pdf" or ".dwg" extension regardless of revision. 
(i.e. xxxx.pdf) 
1.3.5. All documents created electronically shall b e submitted in black and white ".pdf" format. 
Native files converted to ".pdf" shall be forced to black in order to avoid illegible (grayed area) output of 
color text / lines of original document. 
1.3.6. Electronic files must be of sufficient quali ty to produce legible prints by high volume 
reproduction machines.

1.3.7. Each individual document shall be submitted as a single e-file. Multi-page ".pdf" files are 
acceptable only when those pages represent a single document that will always be submitted in that form. 
(i.e. a multi-page manual or a set of drawings that will always be submitted as a group even if only one of

1.3.8. All documents, calculations, drawings and da ta shall be written in the English language and all

1.4. Drawing Requirements 
1.4.1. Vendor shall certify each drawing as correct , positively identify it as to the equipment to which it 
applies, and list all changes on each drawing maintaining a date and description of each revision. 
1.4.2. All final drawings shall be sent in ".pdf" a nd ".dwg" formats for the project. Final drawings not 
sent in CAD format will result in payment delays. 
1.4.3. Each drawing shall have the correct CAD "pap er size" noted on the document. (i.e. 11x17, 
8-1/2x11, etc.) 
1.4.4. Vendor shall notify Abener Teyma if any chan ges are made to the final drawings in writing no 
more than 5 days after any design change. This letter should explain the change with pictures & drawings 
and explain why it was necessary. Abener Teyma reserves the right to accept or make 
modification/corrections to this change. Vendor shall be responsible for updating all final manufacturing 
drawings and incorporating these changes into the as-built drawings. 
1.4.5. Bill of material shall show all OEM names, e quipment part numbers, and equipment ratings. 
1.5. Software File Requirements

1.5.2. All software reports shall meet the requirem ents of Section 1.3: Documents Requirements.
