1. PROJECT DESCRIPTION 
 
1.1 The overall Solana Generating Station project consi sts of “greenfield” plant site near 
Gila Bend, Arizona. A new solar power generating p lant will be built at the site. 
Vendor shall determine the optimum equipment requir ed to meet the process 
requirements set forth in this specification. Vend or is to provide a quotation for 
equipment based on a 30 year life. 
 
2. FUNCTION 
 
2.1 To maintain equipment integrity by providing suffic ient fluid relief at the designated 
pressure levels. 
 
3. OPERATION 
 
3.1 Safety Valves shall be designed to meet all design and climatic conditions in this 
Specification. 
3.2 Vendor shall ensure that Set Pressure of safety val ves does not vary with varying back 
pressure. 
 
4. SCHEDULE 
 
4.1 Short schedule duration has been projected for this project. Vendor shall propose the 
best available delivery dates. 
 
5. GENERAL: 
 
5.1 Valves shall be tested and pressure set by Manufacturer in accordance with ASME Boiler 
and Pressure Vessel Code, Section VIII, Division 1, and shall be National Board certified.

atmosphere, handling atmospheric air, or in contact with process streams (Heat Transfer 
Fluid (Therminol VP-1), steam, Cooling Water, Nitrogen). Vendor must identify all items
