1. General 
1.1 This specification describes the scope of work for supply of all instruments as listed in this request for quote needed for the project. 
1.2 Definitions 
1.2.1 "Supplier" shall mean the instrument supply company chosen for the project.

1.2.3 "Fabricator" or "fabricators" shall mean any instrument assembly shop subcontracted by Abener Teyma (if applicable). 
1.2.4 "Owner" shall mean Abengoa Solar Inc. 
1.3 The attachments identified in this document are considered part of this specification and Supplier shall be bound to adhere to these 
specifications. Any specifications attached shall be met and followed by Supplier in full. All drawings and documents included in this package are 
considered "Contract Documents".

described unless other parties are specifically identified as performing the work. All directives such as "provide", "install", "perform", "test", etc., 
are addressed solely to the Supplier, whether or not the word "Supplier" is specifically mentioned. Work included for others will be specifically 
identified.
