1. General 
 
1.1 This specification sets forth acceptable standards of materials for miscellaneous insulation items. 
Miscellaneous insulation items shall be used to form and provide a complete insulation system. 
 
1.2 This specification is an integral part of specifications 4521-ESP-ANC-00-62-0002, Insulation 
Subcontract.
