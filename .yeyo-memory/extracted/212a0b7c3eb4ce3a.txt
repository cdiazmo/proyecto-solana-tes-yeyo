Notes:
1. Threaded connection fittings may be used at equipment or specialty items as required. These connections should be kept to a minimum 
use. Schedule 80 pipe is to be used for threaded connections.
2. All threaded connections to be made up with TFE thread seal tape (by field).
3. Cabot Type 80P PVC solvent cement for socket joints (or approved equal) with manufacturer ’s detailed instructions on cleanliness of joint, 
amount of joint cement to use, acceptable temperature, set up time before handling and testing are to be supplied with material for field 
assembly.
4. PVC lines can be electric traced, but not steam traced. In no case should temperature of piping system exceed 120  F.
5. Pipe spool may be required to prevent interference between disc and other specialty items upon operation of valve.
6. Use of washers is mandatory under bolt heads and nuts. Washers per ANSI B18.22.1, Table 1A Size N (narrow).
7. All above ground PVC pipi ng exposed to direct sunlight shall be painted per spec 4521 -ESP-ANC-00-60-N101 as UV protection.
