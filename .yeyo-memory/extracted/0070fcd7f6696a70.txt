Notes: Notes: Notes: Notes: 
1. All cut groove pipe, couplings, bolts and gaskets (except at flanged connections) in sizes 3” through 24” shall be per Victualic Co. of 
America’s cut groove piping method for standard steel piping, ITT-Grinnell Corp.’s “Gruvlok” cut groove piping system for standard steel 
piping, Aeroquip Corp.’s (Gustin-Bacon piping products) “Gruvagrip” cut groove piping system or approved equal. Lubricants for 
coupling gaskets and installation instructions are to be supplied by vendor per manufacturer’s recommendations. 
2. Valve CC43W to be used for potable water only. 
3. Use TFE paste on all screwed connections. Paste to be “Bakerseal” #899-21, Baker Oil Tools, Inc. Locktite #59231 or equal (furnished by 
field). 
4. Threadolets or weldolets are preferred at tie-in locations regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting. 
5. Reducing bushings shall not be used. Use reducing couplings or swaged nipples. 
6. All closed cooling water piping shall , at a minimum, have all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas 
(“TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process.
