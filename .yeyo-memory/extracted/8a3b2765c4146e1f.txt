Special Considerations: For valve descriptions, see Standard PS -V350 (block) and PS-V351 (butterfly).
1. Cut groove piping will be used only as detailed on piping drawings and may not be used when bromine or hydrocarbons detrimental to EP DM are present. When shown on the detail 
piping drawing, cut groove pipe, couplings, bolts and gaskets (except at flanged connections) in sizes 3 ” thru 24” and shall be per Victualic Co. of America ’s cut groove piping method 
for standard steel piping, ITT - Grinnel Corp ’s. “gruvlok” cut groove piping system for standard steel piping, Aeroquip Corporations (Gustin -Bacon piping products) “Gruvagrip” cut 
groove piping system or approved equal. Lubricants for coupling gaskets and installation instructions are t o be supplied by vendor per manufacturer ’s recommendations. Cut groove 
piping (3” thru 24” only) is intended to be used only in pipe racks or on other long straight runs where fittings and changes of direction are minimized.
2. All screwed connections to be made up with TFE thread seal tape (by field).
3. Threadolet or stub-in is preferred for use at tie-in location regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting.
4. Pipe spool may be required to prevent interferences between disc and other specialty items upon operation of valve.
