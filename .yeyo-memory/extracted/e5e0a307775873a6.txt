Notes:
1. Welding must be in accordance with ANSI B31.3. Welds at front & back of slip -on flange to be made with 308 or 309 welding rod. Fillet 
weld at pipe end must be uniformly flush with flange face.
2. In sizes ¾” thru 4”, 1.5 diameter cold bends made from pipe may be used as an alterna tive to buttweld fittings. These bends must 
conform to ANSI B31.3.
3. Stub ends are to be used only as required to assure proper alignment at odd -angle piping configuration. 
4. ½” pipe (or ½” OD tubing - 0.065 inch min. wall) will be permitted only between inst ruments and the valves adjacent to the instruments. 
5. Shop and field welds shall be made b using inert gas shielded arc welding in order to minimize contamination of the inside of the pipe. 
Use of backup rings is prohibited. Interior of pipe at weld shall b e wire brushed, wiped clean and swabbed with oil compatible to the 
system where possible.
6. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per spec PS -010.
7. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum standard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, cons truction debris and foreign objects.
8. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas ( “TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process.
