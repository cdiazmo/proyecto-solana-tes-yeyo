1. General 
1.1 This specification describes the scope of work for supply of all instruments as listed in this request for quote needed for the project. 
1.2 Definitions
1.2.1 "Supplier" shall mean the instrument supply company chosen for the project.
1.2.2 "Customer" shall mean Abener Teyma.
1.2.3 "Fabricator" or "fabricators" shall mean any instrument assembly shop subcontracted by Abener Teyma (if applicable).
1.2.4 "Owner" shall mean Abengoa Solar Inc.
1.3 The attachments identified in this document are considered part of this specification and Supplier shall be bound to adhere to these specifications. 
Any specifications attached shall be met and followed by Supplier in full. All drawings and documents included in this package are considered 
"Contract Documents".
1.4 The imperative language of this specification is directed to the Supplier, unless specifically noted otherwise. Supplier shall perform all work 
described unless other parties are specifically identified as performing the work. All directives such as "provide", "install", "perform", "test", etc., are 
addressed solely to the Supplier, whether or not the word "Supplier" is specifically mentioned. Work included for others will be specifically identified.
1.5 English/ U.S. units shall be used on all drawings, calculations and any other documentation submitted by Supplier.

gasket, manifolds … etc as identified in the main specification as options or accessories. Shipping schedule to be confirm with purchasing during the 
final commercial agreement

Supplier of material required on an as needed basis. Shipments to Fabricator or jobsite should be scheduled to ensure a full load will be delivered in 
order to reduce transportation costs.
