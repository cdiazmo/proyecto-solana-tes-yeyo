Notes:
1. All screwed connections at specialty items to be made up with TFE thread seal tape (by field).
2. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie-in locations regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting.
3. KE40T (4” - 12”) valves are not to be used on tapped connections due to possibility of valve flange having tapped holes at bonnet area.
4. Pipe spool may be required to prevent interferences between disc and other specialty items upon operation of valve.
5. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas ( “TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process.
