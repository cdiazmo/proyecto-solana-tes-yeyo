Notes:
1. All screwed connections to be made up with TFE thread seal tape (by field).
2. TA1 (CL 150 screwed gate valve) may be used with screwed Schedule 80 nipples for vents, PI connections, drains and orifice taps. (Field 
fab.).
3. Use blind gasket at all blind flange locations.
4. Sockolets, weldolets or stub-ins with reinforcement pad (indicated on drawings) as required per ANSI B31.3 are preferred at tie-in 
locations regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting.
5. Pipe spool may be required to prevent interferences between disc and other specialty items upon operation of valve.
