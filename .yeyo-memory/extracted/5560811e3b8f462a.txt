2 INSTALLATION SITE
2.1 Location
These vessels shall be erected for the Solana Generation Station. The plant site is located in Maricopa
County, Arizona, approximately 80 miles southwest of Phoenix, and
20 miles west of the town on Gila Bend near Interstate 8 and Painted Rock Dam Road.
2.2 Jurisdictional Authority
2.2.1 National Legislation Applicable
The equipment shall comply with all American regulations, laws and requirements. Local, regional,
national (USA) requirements shall be used for the determination and application of all loads.
2.3 Environmental Conditions

Wind Loads according to ASCE 7-05 Code.
The following parameters will be considered:
- Basic Wind Speed: 90 MPH
- Wind Exposure Category: C
- Wind Importance Factor: IW = 1.15 for Category III (central plant)
- Wind Directionality Factor: KD = 0.95 for round chimneys and tanks
- Topographic Factor: KZT = 1.0
- Gust Effect Factor: G = 0.85 (for rigid structures)

7 DESIGN FATIGUE LIFE

A fatigue analysis is required for this vessel as the intended vessel operation does not satisfy

the Requirement of Clause 5.5.2. of Part-5 of ASME code section VIII Div. 2:

(1) This pressure vessel is not subjected to fatigue due to pressure (constant pressure in both
sides).

(2) The total number of full range pressure cycles and operating pressure cycles (including
startup and shutdown), plus those in which the range of pressure variation exceeds 20% of
the design pressure, is below 1000 cycles.

(3) The only source of fatigue cycles is metal temperature variations (thermal cycles).
