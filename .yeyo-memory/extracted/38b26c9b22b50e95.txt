1. Project Description 
Refer to Specification 4521-ESP-ANC-00-62-0001, Project Design Criteria 
 
2. Purpose 
 
2.1 This specification defines the scope of work , insulating materials, and guidelines for the 
application of external insulation and weatherproofed protective cover systems onto heat traced 
piping and equipment in a solar plant for the purpose of conserving heat and maintaining 
process temperatures. 
 
2.2 Mineral wool is the basis for the design. Details for material can be found in the attachments of 
this specification.

specifically noted otherwise. Contractor shall perform all work described unless others are
