Notes: Notes: Notes: Notes: 
1. All piping shall comply with ANSI B31.3 basic code (fabrication and erection, Sectio n 327; inspection and examination, Section 340 and 341) 
unless otherwise specified in the shop fabrication specification. 
2. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie -in locations regardless of size change due to diff iculty of realignment of piping after 
cutting. 
3. In nitrate salt service, the minimum pipe size shall be 2 inches. 
4. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per spec 4521-ESP-ANC-00-61-PS010. 
5. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas (“TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process. 
6. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum standard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, construction debris and foreign objects.
