Notes: Notes: Notes: Notes: 
1. Screwed connections are only allowed for fit up to screwed connection equipment. These connections to be made up with TFE Thread 
Seal Tape (by field). 
2. Weldolets are preferred for use at tie-in locations regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after cutting 
(minimum size ¾”). 
3. TE50T (4” - 12”) Valves are not to be used on tapped connections due to possibility of valve flange having tapped holes at bonnet area. 
4. Examination in accordance with ASME B31.3 Paragraph 341.4.1. 
5. All fabrication welds shall have GTAW (TIG) process for the root pass. 
6. Spring loaded check valves are only to be used as indicated on the P&IDs.
