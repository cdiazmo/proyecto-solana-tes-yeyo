2. CPVC material becomes quite brittle at temperatures below 40 ° F. Electric Heat Tracing should be considered when temperatures are 
expected to be normally below this limit. 
3. When threaded connections are required, make up joints using TFE tape. Threaded connections are to be held to an absolute minimum. 
All socketweld joints must be cleaned (primed) with manufacturer’s recommended primer prior to application of solvent cement. 
Solvent cement must be applied and handled in strict accordance with manufacturer’s directions. 
4. Pipe spool may be required to prevent interference between disc and other specialty items upon operation of the valve. 
5. Use of washers is mandatory under bolt head and nuts. Washers per ANSI B18.22.1, Table 1A Size N (Narrow).
