General Notes
1.- The complete definition and codification of the supports for construction are included in "0000-LIS-AEE-000-063-YYYY_Rev00_Support List".
2.- General pipe parameters as diameter, loads, orientation and displacements, required for support calculation or its validation, are included in the Support
List.
3.- Please check "comments" in the "typical support" sheet inside the mentioned support list for clarifications or special considerations about typical support.
4.- If not exist a special definition of the material, all elements of this catalogue will be carbon steel S 275JR G2 or similar. For shoes to be mounted in alloy
steel pipe, these shoes will be fabricated in alloy steel P11 or P22 to value by the supplier. For a different material considered in this standard, will be included
a note in comments. The auxiliaries structures of the supports will take a cover hot galvanized with a minimum 85 microns.
5.- Dimensions in inches.
6.- All the diameters are in inches according ASME B36.10.
7.- The position and orientation of the support will be defined by the reference point indicated bracket on the isometric.
8.- For items that must be designed by the supplier, the modification by the supplier of the design parameters should be
approved by the responsible of EPC.
9.-The pipes shall be temporarily supported during installation until the final support has been installed
10.- All springs must remain blocked until the hydrostatic test has been realized.
11.- All shoes will fabricated for the installation of non-rotating trunnion but only non-rotating trunnion will be installed when the support is not simple
support (see typical P12).
12.- Indications weld thickness:
12.2.- Welding and heat treatment of elements supports directly welded pipes are fabricated according to ASME B31.1 Chapter V.
