Notes: Notes: Notes: Notes: 
1. All piping shall comply with ANSI B31.3 basic code (fabrication and erection, Section 327; inspection and examination, Sectio n 340 and 
341) unless otherwise specified in the shop fabrication specification. 
2. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie -in locations regardless of size change due to diffi culty of realignment of piping after 
cutting. 
3. Flanges shall be used only where required to mate with equipment nozzles. All other joints shall be buttweld connections. 
4. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per spec 4521-ESP-ANC-00-61-PS010. 
5. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas (“TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process. 
6. All shop welds to receive 5% X-ray. All field welds to receive 100% X-ray. 
7. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum standard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, construction debris and foreign objects.
