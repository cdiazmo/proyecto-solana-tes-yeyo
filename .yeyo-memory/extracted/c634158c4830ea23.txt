Calculation Volume Criteria:
- The internal Volume of the Fittings are calculated taking account his wall thickness excepting fittings without Schedule or thickness in his description.
- Fittings with two different Schedules (inlet and outlet) are calculated with the inlet (DN1) schedule.
- Reducers and Tee fitting are calculated taking into consideration the actual geometry.
- Other Fittings are calculated like tube surface (Cilinder).
- This calculation will not be made for plastic specifications (PXXX,VXXX)
Calculation Surface Criteria: 
- Couplings external surfaces was calculated adding 10% to the total surface of the fitting.
- Flanges external surfaces was calculated adding 100% to the total surface of the fitting.
- Reducers and Tee fitting are calculated taking into consideration the actual geometry.
- Other Fittings are calculated like tube surface (Cilinder).
- This calculation will not be made for plastic specifications (PXXX,VXXX)
Welding Number
- For fittings with inlet and outlet, two weldings will be considered (note that if there are two fittings attached then one welding will be counted over). 
- For TEE fittings three weldings will be considered.
- For TUBE elements only will be considered the cuts following these criterias:
Solar field and Diameter <= 2" ---> there will be a cut (welding) every 6 m. 
Solar field and Diameter > 2" ---> there will be a cut (welding) every 11 m. 
Other systems (HTF, TES, SW, Utilities) and Diameter <= 8" --> there will be a cut (welding) every 6 m.
Other systems (HTF, TES, SW, Utilities) and Diameter > 8" --> there will be a cut (welding) every 10 m.
- For CAP fittings only a welding is considered except if it's DRILLED. In this case will be considered two weldings.
- For OLET fittings two weldings will be considered.
- For STUB END fittings will be considered one welding.
- For BEND TUBES, ELBOW, COUPLINGS, REDUCERS fittings will be considered two weldings.
- Cut COUPLINGS for small pipings (>= 2") aren't cosidered in the MTO (unless they are modeled). In this case, only will be considered one welding for the cut
- For FLANGES:
LAP JOINT --> 0 welding
SLIP ON --> 2 weldings
BLIND --> 0 welding
SOCKET --> 1 welding
WELDING NECK --> 1 welding
- Welding valves are considered in the first TUBE or NIPPLE of its Pipe Line that the macro finds at the csv list
Diametral weld inches
- Have been considered all the inlets and outles of each element
Equivalent Weld inches
- This calculation will not be made for plastic specifications (PXXX,VXXX)
- See the coedfficents Sheet
