Notes:
1. Sockolets or weldolets are preferred for use at tie -in location regardless of size change due to difficulty of realignment of piping after 
cutting. 
2. All pipe & fittings to be internally cleaned per spec 4521-ESP-ANC-00-61-PS010.
3. All pipe shall be installed “clean”. The minimum standard shall be free of mill scale, slag, dirt, construction debris and foreign objects.
4. At a minimum, all shop and field root pass welds shall be done using Tungsten Inert Gas ( “TIG” – “Heliarc”) welding process.
